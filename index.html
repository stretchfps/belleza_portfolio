About Me

Hi, I'm Aakash

I like simplicity, naturalness and minimalism in photography. I don't like awkward posing, but it helps me not to get lost. I try to capture moments that are unstaged, real, and the people in them are open.

There is a lot of quiet time together during a photo shoot. My goal is for you to get not only beautiful photos, but also to remember a pleasant photo session experience. I want to give you memories to remind you of the emotions experienced that day, the unseen smiles, and the significant details of the celebration.

I can't wait to meet you! We will meet over a cup of coffee and discuss the details of your celebration :)
